The complexity of fighting traffic

In a world of constant mobility innovations you’d think that eliminating traffic would be simple. It will be with quantum computing.

The case for quantum computers

By 2020, 250 million smart connected vehicles are supposed to roam are streets - that would be 1/5 of all world vehicles

This level of connectivity allows us to have more information about the traffic and then steer cars accordingly. So if you had such data from every car in the city (or at least the majority) you could plan routes for all of them with a high degree of precision

At a lower level if you had such data for (just) an entire fleet (all taxis, buses, delivery vehicles), you could improve the route planning for those.
